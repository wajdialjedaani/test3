How to Use Mindful Moments

Getting Started

Mindful Moments is an accessible meditation timer designed to help you practice mindfulness.

Setting Up Your Session

  1. Set your desired meditation duration (1-120 minutes)
  2. Optionally set interval bells to chime during your session
  3. Choose a background ambient sound (optional)
  4. Adjust the volume to your preference

During Meditation

Press the Start button to begin your session. You can pause at any time if needed. The progress bar will show how much of your session is complete.

After Meditation

When your session completes, you'll be prompted to record how you feel. Your session will be saved to your meditation history.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Start/Pause meditation
  • R - Reset timer
  • Esc - Close any open modal

Accessibility Features

This application is designed to be fully accessible with screen readers, keyboard navigation, and high contrast visuals. If you encounter any accessibility issues, please contact us.
