Help & Information

How to Use This Countdown Timer

This countdown timer shows the days, hours, minutes, and seconds remaining until your event. The progress bar indicates how much time has passed relative to the total waiting period.

Customizing Your Countdown

To set up a countdown for your own event, fill out the form above with your event details. The countdown will automatically update to reflect your new event date and time.

Accessibility Features

This countdown timer is fully accessible and includes keyboard navigation, screen reader support, and high contrast options. Press Tab to navigate between interactive elements and Enter to activate them.
