My Herb Garden Transformation

HerbGardener
Posted 5 days ago

I've been working on transforming my herb garden for the past month, and I'm finally ready to share the results! I built three raised beds and organized my herbs by watering needs to make maintenance easier.

In the first bed (red), I planted basil, which loves consistent moisture. The middle bed (green) contains rosemary, which prefers drier conditions. The last bed (blue) has mint, which I contained in its own space to prevent it from spreading everywhere.

This organization has made caring for my herbs so much easier, and they're all thriving! I'm already harvesting fresh herbs for cooking almost every day.
