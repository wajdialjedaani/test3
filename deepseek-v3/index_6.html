Vegetable Stir Fry

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 10 min | Serves: 4 Category: Vegetarian

Description

A quick and healthy vegetable stir fry with a delicious garlic soy sauce. Perfect for busy weeknights!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add bell pepper, carrot, and broccoli. Stir fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add snap peas and continue cooking for 2 more minutes.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.
  6. Pour sauce over vegetables and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Cook for another minute until sauce thickens slightly.
  8. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds before serving.
