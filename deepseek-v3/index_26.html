Help & Guidance

Learn how to make the most of your meditation practice.

Meditation is a practice where an individual uses a technique – such as mindfulness, or focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity – to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.

To begin:

  1. Find a quiet, comfortable place to sit
  2. Set your timer for a manageable duration (5-10 minutes to start)
  3. Close your eyes and focus on your breath
  4. When your mind wanders (it will), gently bring your focus back to your breath
  5. Be kind to yourself - meditation is a practice, not perfection

The meditation timer is designed to support your practice with these features:

  • Custom Duration: Start with shorter sessions (5-10 minutes) and gradually increase as you become more comfortable.
  • Interval Bells: These gentle reminders can help bring your focus back if your mind has wandered.
  • Ambient Sounds: Experiment with different sounds to find what helps you focus best.
  • Volume Control: Adjust the volume so the sounds are present but not distracting.

If you're experiencing issues with the timer:

  • Ensure your browser is up to date
  • Check that your device volume is not muted
  • Refresh the page if the timer becomes unresponsive
  • For persistent issues, try clearing your browser cache

If problems continue, please contact support.
