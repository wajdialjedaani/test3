Help Center

How to Use TaskMaster Pro

TaskMaster Pro helps you organize your daily tasks and earn rewards for completing them.

Adding Tasks

Click the "Add New Task" button to create a new task. Fill in the details including title, description, due date, priority, and point value.

Completing Tasks

Mark tasks as complete by clicking the checkmark button next to each task. Completed tasks earn you points.

Earning Rewards

As you accumulate points, you can redeem them for rewards in the Rewards section.

Keyboard Navigation
  • Use Tab to navigate between interactive elements
  • Press Enter or Space to activate buttons
  • Use Escape to close modals
