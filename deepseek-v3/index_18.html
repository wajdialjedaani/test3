About Me

Hello! I'm Jane, a passionate UX designer with over 8 years of experience creating accessible digital experiences. I specialize in inclusive design and accessibility compliance, helping organizations build products that everyone can use.

My journey in design began when I realized how many digital products excluded people with disabilities. Since then, I've made it my mission to advocate for accessibility in every project I work on.

When I'm not designing, you can find me hiking in the mountains, reading science fiction, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.
